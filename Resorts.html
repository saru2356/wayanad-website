Rooz Green Leaf

Featuring a garden, terrace and views of mountain, Rooz Green Leaf is located in Kalpetta, 37 km from Soochipara Falls. The resort has garden views, an outdoor pool and a 24-hour front desk. All rooms at the resort feature a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, Blu-ray player, and a private bathroom with a shower, a hairdryer and free toiletries. The rooms are equipped with an electric tea pot, while some rooms also feature a kitchen with a dishwasher, a fridge and an oven. Guest rooms include a desk. Continental and à la carte breakfast options are available every morning at Rooz Green Leaf. The accommodation provides an ironing service, as well as business facilities like fax and photocopying. The nearest airport is Kannur International, 82.1 km from Rooz Green Leaf, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service. We speak your language!

Tea tours Ripon Heritage Bungalow

Tea tours Ripon Heritage Bungalow is set in Meppadi amid the lush tea plantations of Wayanad. Boasting a 24-hour front desk, this property also features a restaurant and a sun terrace. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 10 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
